Description

Appearance:
The 1 Mukhi Rudraksha bead is characterized by seventeen natural divisions or faces on its surface. It typically has a rounded shape with distinct and well-defined lines, symbolizing auspiciousness and spiritual potency.
...
Symbolism:
This sacred bead is associated with Lord Vishvakarma, the divine architect and craftsman of the gods in Hindu mythology. It is believed to bestow blessings of creativity, innovation, and divine protection upon its wearer.
